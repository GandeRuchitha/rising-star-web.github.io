Group Lessons

Students come together to learn or practice coding to create software, mobile apps, websites, and other computer programs. The group setting allows for students to learn from each other, collaborate and share their knowledge. Group classes are led by an instructor who are experienced in the specific coding language or technology being taught.

  • Improve Social Skills
  • Pool Knowledge and Ideas
  • Share Diverse Perspectives
