Group Classes

Students come together to learn or practice coding.

Diversity of Perspectives

In a group setting, students can learn from each other and gain different perspectives, which can enhance their overall learning experience.

Develop Teamwork Skills

Group classes provide opportunities for students to develop teamwork skills, which can be beneficial in both academic and professional settings.

Competition

Healthy competition can motivate individuals to push themselves to do better and achieve their goals.

Social Interactions

Group classes provide opportunities for students to meet new people and interact with others who share similar interests.

Support System

Group classes offer a support system for students who may be struggling with certain concepts or skills. Students can learn from their peers and receive encouragement and feedback from the instructor.

Encourages Active Learning

Group settings encourage students to actively participate in the learning process, as they can ask questions, offer input, and engage in discussions with others.
